1. Aizawl.

Aizawl is the capital city of the northeastern state of Mizoram, India. It is located in the midst of hills and is known for its scenic beauty, vibrant culture, and delicious food. The city is home to several tourist attractions like the Mizoram State Museum, Durtlang Hills, and Solomon's Temple. The city has a vibrant market and a bustling street life, offering a unique experience to visitors. Aizawl is also known for its rich cultural heritage and traditional handloom and handicraft products. It is a must-visit destination for travelers who want to explore the rich culture and natural beauty of Mizoram..




2. Lunglei.

Lunglei is a town located in the southern part of Mizoram, India. It is known for its picturesque landscapes, serene surroundings, and unique culture. The town is home to several natural attractions like the Khawnglung Wildlife Sanctuary, Kawmzawl Wildlife Sanctuary, and Saza Wildlife Sanctuary. The town is also famous for its traditional handloom products, which are sold in the local market. Lunglei is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts who want to explore the rich flora and fauna of Mizoram. The town offers a unique blend of culture and nature, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.
