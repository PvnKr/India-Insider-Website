1. Jagannath Mandhir.

Jagannath Mandir is a beautiful temple located in the city of Ranchi, in the Indian state of Jharkhand. The temple is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu, and is renowned for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings. The temple is an important pilgrimage site for Hindus, and it attracts devotees from all over the world who come to seek the blessings of Lord Jagannath. The temple complex comprises several smaller temples and shrines dedicated to various deities, and it is surrounded by lush greenery that adds to its beauty and serenity. The main attraction of the temple is the beautiful idol of Lord Jagannath, which is intricately carved and adorned with precious jewels. Visitors can attend the daily puja and aarti, which are conducted with great devotion and reverence. The temple also hosts several festivals throughout the year, including the annual Rath Yatra, which is a grand celebration that attracts thousands of devotees. Jagannath Mandir is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in Hindu mythology and spirituality, and it is sure to leave visitors with a sense of peace and serenity.


2. Patratu Valley.

Patratu Valley is a scenic valley located in the Indian state of Jharkhand, near the city of Ranchi. It is surrounded by lush green forests and rolling hills, and it offers breathtaking views of the natural beauty of the region. The valley is situated on the banks of the Patratu River, and it is renowned for its stunning waterfalls, pristine lakes and majestic hills. Visitors can enjoy a range of activities such as hiking, trekking, and bird watching, and they can also explore nearby attractions such as the Ranchi Hill and Hundru Falls. The valley is also home to several traditional villages, where visitors can experience the local culture and way of life. The valley is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, and it offers a refreshing break from the hustle and bustle of city life. Patratu Valley is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a peaceful and rejuvenating holiday amidst stunning natural surroundings.



3.Dhanbad.

Dhanbad is a vibrant city located in the Indian state of Jharkhand, known for its rich history and culture, as well as its thriving industries. The city is situated in the heart of the coal belt of India and is home to several coal mines and industries. Despite being an industrial city, Dhanbad also boasts several natural and cultural attractions that are worth exploring. Visitors can explore nearby attractions such as the Maithon Dam, Topchanchi Lake, and the Panchet Dam, which offer stunning views and opportunities for outdoor activities. The city also has several historical landmarks, including the Bhatinda Falls and the Jagannath Temple, which are popular tourist destinations. The city is also known for its delectable street food, which offers a range of culinary delights that are sure to tantalize the taste buds of visitors. Dhanbad is a bustling and lively city that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and modernity, making it a must-visit destination for anyone interested in exploring the diversity of India.




4.Dassam Falls.

Dassam Falls is a beautiful waterfall located in the Indian state of Jharkhand, near the city of Ranchi. The waterfall is situated on the Kanchi River, and it cascades down from a height of 144 feet, creating a breathtaking spectacle that is sure to leave visitors in awe. The name "Dassam" means ten, and it refers to the ten streams that converge to form the waterfall. The area surrounding the waterfall is covered in lush greenery, and visitors can enjoy a range of activities such as hiking, trekking, and picnicking. The waterfall is a popular tourist destination, and visitors can also explore nearby attractions such as the Jonha Falls and Hundru Falls, which are equally stunning. The best time to visit Dassam Falls is during the monsoon season, when the waterfall is at its full force and the surrounding greenery is at its most vibrant. Dassam Falls is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and anyone looking for a refreshing break amidst stunning natural surroundings.



5.Canary Hill Hazaribagh.

Canary Hill is a beautiful hill station located in the Indian state of Jharkhand, near the city of Hazaribagh. The hill station is situated at an altitude of 615 meters above sea level, and it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys. The area surrounding Canary Hill is covered in lush greenery, and visitors can enjoy a range of activities such as hiking, trekking, and bird watching. The hill station is also known for its pleasant climate, which makes it a popular destination for tourists looking to escape the scorching heat of the plains. Visitors can explore nearby attractions such as the Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary and the Rajrappa Waterfalls, which are equally stunning. The hill station is also home to several colonial-era bungalows and churches, which are worth visiting for their historical significance and architectural beauty. Canary Hill is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and anyone looking for a peaceful and rejuvenating holiday amidst stunning natural surroundings.
