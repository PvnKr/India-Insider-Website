2. Dudhsagar Falls.

Welcome to Dudhsagar Falls, one of the most magnificent waterfalls in India, located in Goa. The name "Dudhsagar" translates to "sea of milk," and rightfully so, as the waterfall appears like a milky white cascade against the lush greenery of the Western Ghats. Surrounded by dense forests and beautiful scenery, Dudhsagar Falls is a popular tourist destination and a must-visit for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. 4. Butterfly Beach.

Welcome to Butterfly Beach, a hidden gem and one of the most picturesque beaches in Goa, India. Located in the southern part of Goa, Butterfly Beach is a secluded and tranquil destination that is accessible only by boat. With its clear blue waters, soft white sands, and beautiful rock formations, the beach is a paradise for nature lovers and photographers. The beach is also home to a diverse array of flora and fauna, including several species of butterflies, thus giving it its name.
