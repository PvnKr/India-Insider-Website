1. Kurukshetra.

Kurukshetra is a historic city located in the northern Indian state of Haryana. It is considered one of the most sacred places in India and is believed to be the site of the famous battle between the Kauravas and Pandavas from the Hindu epic, Mahabharata. The city is home to several ancient temples, including the famous Bhadrakali temple and the Sri Krishna Museum. Kurukshetra is also known for its beautiful lakes, parks, and gardens, making it a popular destination for tourists and pilgrims. The city's rich cultural and historical significance makes it a must-visit destination for anyone interested in Indian history and spirituality.




2. Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary is a popular tourist destination located in Gurgaon district of Haryana, India. It is home to over 250 species of resident and migratory birds, making it a paradise for birdwatchers and nature lovers. The sanctuary was established in 1972 and covers an area of approximately 143 acres. The best time to visit is between November and February, when the migratory birds arrive. The sanctuary offers visitors a chance to see a wide variety of birds, including ducks, geese, herons, and egrets, among others. The beautiful natural surroundings and peaceful atmosphere make Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary a must-visit destination for anyone visiting Haryana.




3. Gurgaon.

Gurgaon, also known as Gurugram, is a city located in the Indian state of Haryana, just southwest of the national capital, New Delhi. It is one of the fastest-growing cities in India and is a hub for several multinational companies, making it a major commercial and financial center. Gurgaon is also known for its modern infrastructure, shopping malls, and entertainment facilities. The city is home to several tourist attractions, including the Kingdom of Dreams, the Leisure Valley Park, and the Sheetla Mata Temple. With its modern facilities, vibrant culture, and thriving economy, Gurgaon is a must-visit destination for anyone visiting Haryana.




4. Panipat.

Panipat is a historic city located in the Indian state of Haryana. It is known for its pivotal role in several significant battles throughout Indian history, including the three famous battles of Panipat. The city is home to several historic landmarks, including the famous Panipat Museum and the Kabuli Bagh Mosque. Panipat is also known for its beautiful temples, such as the Devi Temple and the Kala Amb Temple. The city's rich history, cultural heritage, and beautiful natural surroundings make it a popular destination for tourists and history enthusiasts. With its deep roots in Indian history, Panipat is a must-visit destination for anyone visiting Haryana.




5. Yadavindra Gardens.

Auli is a popular ski destination located in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, India. It is situated at an altitude of 2,500 to 3,050 meters above sea level and offers stunning views of the Himalayan mountain range. Auli is a perfect destination for adventure seekers and skiing enthusiasts. It is surrounded by coniferous and oak forests, making it a picturesque destination for nature lovers. Auli also offers various winter sports activities like skiing, snowboarding, and trekking. It has some of the best ski slopes in the country and is often referred to as the "Skiing Capital of India." Overall, Auli is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for an adrenaline-filled adventure in the lap of nature.
